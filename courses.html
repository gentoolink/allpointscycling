Our Courses

Comprehensive cycling safety training for professional riders

🐢

Low-Speed Maneuvers

Master balance and control at low speeds — essential for navigating checkpoints, crowds, and confined spaces with precision and confidence.

🚦

Advanced Traffic Safety

Defensive riding techniques, traffic positioning, hazard recognition, and the skills needed to navigate complex urban environments safely.

🏔️

Obstacle Course Mastery

Conquer curbs, gravel, potholes, and uneven surfaces. Build the reflexes and techniques to handle any terrain without losing control.

🚪

Confined Space Operation

Maneuver effectively in tight spaces — elevators, narrow corridors, building entrances, and event venues. Critical for security roles.

⚠️

Emergency Procedures

Emergency braking, falling techniques, crash recovery, and situational response. Know how to act when things go wrong on the road.

🚚

Bicycle Trailer Handling

Specialized training for riders operating with cargo trailers. Hitching, load balance, turning, and stopping with attached trailers.

🌙

Nocturnal Riding

Ride safely after dark. Lighting systems, visibility gear, night-time road awareness, and low-traction condition handling.

⚡

E-Bike Expertise

Understand the unique handling, acceleration, and braking of electric bicycles. Battery management, legal requirements, and safe e-bike operation.
