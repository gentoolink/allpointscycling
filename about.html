About All Points Cycling

Dedicated to cycling safety and skill development across British Columbia

Our Foundation

Our Mission

To equip every cyclist — from municipal workers to law enforcement volunteers — with the skills and confidence needed to ride safely and effectively in any environment.

Our Vision

A community where every professional cyclist returns home safely, where cycling is respected as a serious skill, and where riders are treated as competent operators on the road.

Our Values

Discipline, safety, and respect. We believe cycling is not a casual pursuit — it demands the same professionalism and attention as any other skilled trade or public safety role.

Chuck Glover

Lead Instructor & Founder

Chuck Glover brings over 40 years of experience in policing to the world of cycling instruction. His career in law enforcement provided him with extensive training in tactical mobility, defensive driving, and situational awareness — skills that translate directly to safe cycling practices.

As a former RCMP officer, Chuck developed his cycling expertise through years of patrol work, event security, and specialized assignments requiring bicycle mobility. His testimony as an expert court witness in cycling-related incidents gives him unique insight into how accidents happen — and how to prevent them.

Beyond his professional credentials, Chuck is deeply embedded in the local cycling community. He serves as President of Tri M Sports, a role that reflects his ongoing commitment to cycling safety in British Columbia and across Canada.
